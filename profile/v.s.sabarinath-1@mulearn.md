## ⚽ FIFA World Cup Corner


#### My Nation & Why:

- Representing **Germany** in μFIFA, because German football represents discipline, structure, teamwork, and resilience. Their tactical approach and fighting spirit are qualities that I admire both in football and in life.
#### Supporting Team in the Real World Cup 2026:

- Germany, without a doubt.

#### All-Time Favourite Player:

- **Manuel Neuer**, the goalkeeper who completely changed the position. His sweeper-keeper style inspired me to become a goalkeeper myself. His confidence in coming out of the box, controlling the game, organizing the defense, and acting as an extra playmaker showed that goalkeepers can influence the entire match, not just save shots.
#### Best Player Right Now:

- **Lamine Yamal.**, because of his extraordinary talent, creativity, and confidence at such a young age. If he continues developing at the current pace, he has the potential to become one of the greatest players of his generation.
#### Past World Cup Memories:

- **Germany 7–1 Brazil, 2014.** Watching one of the most shocking matches in football history remains unforgettable. It showed how discipline, teamwork, and tactical execution can overcome even the biggest football nations.

#### 2026 Predictions:

- **Winner:** Germany, hopefully. If not, Portugal because it could be Cristiano Ronaldo's final World Cup, and he remains one of my football idols.
- **Dark horse:** Morocco.
